Ctrl + Alt + T || Open terminal

Ctrl + Z || Send to background (suspended). & option sends program to background.
fg || Bring to foreground
Ctrl + D || Log out of terminal
Ctrl + L || Clear terminal screen

Ctrl + A || Cursor to beginning of line
Ctrl + E || Cursor to end of line

Ctrl + U || Erase to beginning of line
Ctrl + K || Erase to end of line
Ctrl + Y || Undo erase

Ctrl + R || Search command history (and cycle results)
Shift + PgUp/PgDn || Scroll up/down by page 

Alt + B || Back a word
Alt + Shift + F || Forward a word

Ctrl + Alt + F7 || Exit kali virtual console
